Sometimes the food we eat can been grown and shipped from all over. Spinach Salad is a popular every day dish with flavours from countries all over the world. In this investigation we take a closer look at the laborious journey some food takes to end up on our plates, how much milk it takes to produce cheese and why we should stop wasting so much bread.
